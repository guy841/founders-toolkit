## Description (4000)
```
Helm is the toolkit every UK limited company director wishes they'd been handed on day one — the deadlines, taxes and duties of running a company, in one calm, private app.

No account. No sign-up. Nothing you enter leaves your device.

WHAT'S INSIDE

• Deadline Copilot — every filing and payment deadline for your company (annual accounts, Corporation Tax, confirmation statement, VAT, PAYE, Self Assessment), counted down from today, with one-tap export to your calendar.

• Corporation Tax Estimator — enter your profit and see what you owe, including the 19% and 25% rates and the marginal relief in between that most people get wrong.

• Salary vs Dividends Optimiser — find the most tax-efficient way to pay yourself, with support for multiple directors and percentage dividend splits — plus how a director's loan compares.

• Company Records — keep your company number, UTR, VAT and PAYE references, insurance and bank details in one place, optionally protected by a passphrase. Stored only on your device.

• New Company Setup Checklist — a personalised list of the registrations, insurances and legal duties a new company needs, including the ones nobody warns you about.

• Insurance Needs Check — see which cover is required by law versus simply advised, based on what your business actually does.

• VAT Registration Checker — track your rolling 12-month turnover against the £90,000 threshold, plus the next-30-days test and voluntary registration.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Everything you enter is saved only on your device. There are no accounts, no tracking and no ads. Company Records can be encrypted with a passphrase you choose.

Helm provides general estimates based on standard UK rules (2025/26 tax year, England, Wales & Northern Ireland) and is not a substitute for professional advice. Always confirm important figures with HMRC, Companies House or a qualified adviser.

Helm is a Tree Top Capital product.
```
