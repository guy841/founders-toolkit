Privacy Policy

Helm, a Tree Top Capital product. Last updated: June 2026.

Helm is a set of tools for UK company directors, available on the web and as an iOS app. This policy explains what happens to your information. In short: your information stays on your device by default, and if you choose to sync it across devices, it is end-to-end encrypted so we still can't read it.

Information you enter

Some tools (such as Company Records, the Setup Checklist and the Insurance Needs Check) let you save details — for example your company number, tax references or insurance information. By default this data is stored only in your browser or app on your own device, using your device's local storage. It is not transmitted to us, and we have no way to see it.

In Company Records you can optionally protect your saved data with a passphrase, which encrypts it on your device. If you choose to do so, keep your passphrase safe — we cannot recover it.

Optional accounts & cross-device sync

You can optionally create an account (with an email address and password) so your saved details sync across your devices. This is entirely opt-in — the tools work fully without it.

If you do, your data is end-to-end encrypted: your details are encrypted on your device with a key only you control before they are uploaded, and our server only ever stores the resulting ciphertext together with your account's email address and the time it was last updated. We — and our hosting provider — cannot read your encrypted information.

When you sign up we give you a one-time recovery key to save. Your password and your recovery key each independently unlock your data, so if you forget your password you can reset it (via an email link) and restore your data with your recovery key. Because we genuinely cannot read your data, if you lose both your password and your recovery key, the data cannot be recovered — by anyone. You can change your password while signed in, and delete your account and all its stored data at any time from within the app.

Accounts and encrypted sync are provided for us by Supabase, which hosts the database and handles sign-in on our behalf. They process your email address and the encrypted blob described above; they cannot decrypt its contents. We store data in the UK/EU region where available.

Information we collect

We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs, and there is no advertising. The calculators perform all their working on your device. If you don't create an account, we collect nothing at all. If you do, the only personal data we hold is your email address and the end-to-end encrypted blob of whatever you chose to save — the contents of which we cannot read.

Third-party services

Helm bundles its own fonts and assets and works offline. We don't use third-party analytics, trackers, advertising, or content-delivery networks. The only external service Helm talks to — and only if you create an account — is Supabase, for sign-in and encrypted sync, as described above.

Children

Helm is intended for company directors and business owners and is not directed at children.

Your data, your control

You are in control of your information. You can clear it at any time from within the tools (for example "Clear this company" in Company Records) or by clearing your browser/app data. Uninstalling the app removes the data stored on that device. If you created an account, you can sign out, change your password, or permanently delete your account and everything synced with it from the account menu — and you can email us if you would like us to confirm deletion.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as Helm evolves. Material changes — particularly to what is stored or how — will be reflected here with a new "last updated" date.
